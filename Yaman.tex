\raag{Yaman}

This raga promotes romanticism, It sounds romantic. Yaman is a \textbf{Shadav - Sampoorna} jaati raag.
The equivalent Raag in Carnatic music is \textbf{kalyani}

\aaroha{\cn{N1RGMDNs2}}

\avaroha{\cn{s2NDPMGRs}}

Thaat: \textbf{Kalyan}

Time: \textbf{7pm to 10pm}

Vadi Swar: \textbf{\Ga}  

Samvadi swar: \textbf{\Nii}

The Pakad of the raag is \textbf{\cn{NRG} \cn{PMGRs}} \textbf{\cn{NRG} \cn{GMPMGRN1D1N1Rs}}
